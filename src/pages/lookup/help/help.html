Help

Date and years

Tap the calendar button to choose the date you want and the amount of past years to lookup. By default this is set for today's date and 10 past years.

Daily precipitation

Adjust the slider to choose the amount of daily precipitation. The app will only consider a day as rainy if the chosen amount of rain (or more) has fallen during that whole day. This might be something you want to set to get a more desirable result. This setting is completely optional and by default it is set for 0 mm.

Worth noting

This setting is only available for lookups 10 past years or less. This is because the older weather data often is to insufficient for such calculations.

Location

Tap the pin button to choose a location. A new window will appear with a search field to type in a location. By default this is set for your current location.

Search

Tap the search button when you are done with all settings to make your lookup. The app will check the chosen date and location for each year as from the year you choose up until {{ lastYear }}. The result will be shown on a new page.
