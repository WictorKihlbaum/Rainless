Help

Basics

Choose a location and a date. By default these are set for your current location and today's date. Tap the search button when you are done with the settings. The app will check the chosen date and location for each year as from the year you choose up until {{ lastYear }}. The result will be shown on a new page.

Date and years

Press the calendar button to choose the date you want and the amount of past years to lookup.

Daily precipitation

Adjust the slider to choose the amount of daily precipitation. The app will only consider a day as rainy if the chosen amount of rain (or more) has fallen during that whole day. This might be something you want to set to get a more desirable result. Often it is enough to set the slider to "1 mm". This is however completely optional and by default it is set for 0 mm.

Worth noting
This option is only available for lookups 10 past years or less. This is because the older weather data often is to insufficient for daily precipitation calculations.

Location

Press the pin button to choose a location. A new window will appear with a search field to type in your location.
